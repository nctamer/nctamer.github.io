About me

I'm a passionate researcher specializing in Music Information Retrieval (MIR), currently pursuing my PhD with Prof. Xavier Serra. My work involves creating innovative algorithms for various MIR tasks, including music transcription, pitch estimation, source separation, and music performance analysis. I explore both self-supervised and weakly-supervised learning approaches, empowering our algorithms to learn from minimal labeled data. Beyond audio, I embrace the synergy of audio, video, and text domains to enhance the richness of music understanding.
